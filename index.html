Welcome to the Talib al-Ilm quiz

This website aims to promote Islam and let students of knowledge test themselves against interactive quizzes of varying difficulty.

This website hosts three separate quizzes of varying difficulty and a leaderboard.


Foundations of Deen (Easy)

A set of ten questions about the foundations of our religion, from the five pillars to tawheed


Islamic History (Intermediate)

A set of twenty questions about all aspects of Islamic history, from the Rashidun Caliphate to the Ottoman Empire


Furu al-Fiqh (Hard)

A set of ten questions on the difficult subject of Islamic jurisprudence


Student Grades (Leaderboard)

A page containing all of the current student high scores
